ABOUT ME

My career path is multifaceted and one of the kind, to put it in a nutshell:
"I’m a pop star turned programmer with a strong background in art and design."
Early in my career, I worked as a singer-songwriter and toured around the world. Transitioning from the entertainment industry to the tech world was a testimony of my determination, inquisitiveness, and motivation. If there’s one thing I learnt from performing on stage, it’s to make a habit of constantly challenging and reinventing myself. Hence, while working as a designer in San Francisco in between record contracts, I delved into the world of coding and I never looked back.
