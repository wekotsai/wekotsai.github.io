ABOUT ME

I’m a full stack engineer with a strong background in design and music. Early in my career I worked as a singer-songwriter and toured around the world. While working in the music industry was immensely rewarding, I wanted to reinvent myself and channeled my creativity into a different field. In 2017, I decided to take the leap and moved to Silicon Valley in between record contracts, where I reconnected my roots in art and design and worked as a graphic designer and illustrator. Eventually, my love for problem-solving and inquisitiveness paved a path in programming, and I never looked back.
